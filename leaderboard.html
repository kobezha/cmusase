Mentorship at SASE

CMU SASE Mentorship aims to strengthen connections and relationships within SASE. New members, called mentees, are encouraged to apply for mentors who aim to share the values of SASE and assist mentees in professionally and in university. Through challenges, SASE activities, volunteer events, and others, mentor and mentee relationships are fostered.
